Smoked Sea Gull

1 seagull 1 cup salt 1 cup sugar water

Directions: in a large non-metalic container combine sugar and salt with enough waterto cover the Sea Gull let sit in brine for 24 hours.

Drain. Let Sea Gull dry on paper towel for 1 hour while preparing smoker. Smoke in electric smoker for 6 hours using 3 pans of saw dust. if air is cool it may take longer for the bird to be cooked through. Test for doneness by moving the birds leg (the leg will move freely)
